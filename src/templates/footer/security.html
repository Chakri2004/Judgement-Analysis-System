Security & Data Protection

At AI Legal, we prioritize the confidentiality, integrity, and security of your legal data. Our platform is designed with strong security practices to ensure safe and reliable document analysis.

Data Encryption

All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS and secure protocols to protect sensitive legal information from unauthorized access.

User Authentication

Secure login system with password hashing ensures that user credentials are never stored in plain text.

AI Processing Safety

Documents are processed securely, and we do not retain confidential content beyond what is necessary for analysis and improvement.

Data Privacy

Your uploaded legal documents are private and accessible only to you. We do not share or sell user data.

Secure Infrastructure

Our backend services are built using secure frameworks and follow best practices to prevent vulnerabilities and attacks.

Regular Updates

We continuously monitor and update our systems to address emerging security threats and improve platform safety.

Need Help?

If you discover any security issue or vulnerability, please contact our support team immediately. We take all reports seriously.
